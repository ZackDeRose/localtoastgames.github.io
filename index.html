Learn More About Me

Welcome to my design portfolio. I'm a passionate web and graphic designer, but I also specialize in web development and branding. I spend most of my day making beautiful interfaces, but I also play pixel basketball over on the courts at Dribbble. I've always been passionate about design so that's why at the early age of fifteen I started to teach myself design. At first I wanted to teach myself graphic design, but a couple of years later I started to get a strong passion for web design. I created my first website in high school, it wasn't very good, but I was excited and it wasn't very long until I wanted to make another one and since then I've never wanted to stop. If you like what you see on my website, then feel free to contact me, I would love to hear from you.
